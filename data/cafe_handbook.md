# Cafe Operations Handbook

## Opening Checks
1. Power on coffee machine and grinder.
2. Run a 5-second group-head flush on each head.
3. Verify milk fridge temperature is between 2C and 5C.
4. Confirm pastry labels and allergen cards are visible.
5. Prepare float and confirm card terminal is online.

## Closing Espresso Machine Cleaning
1. Remove and rinse portafilters and baskets.
2. Backflush each group head with cleaning detergent (one cycle each).
3. Backflush again with clean water (two cycles each).
4. Purge and wipe steam wand after final milk use.
5. Empty and rinse drip tray and knock box.
6. Record cleaning completion in shift checklist.

## Return Policy (Non-food Items)
- Damaged takeaway mugs can be replaced within 7 days of purchase.
- Receipt is preferred but not mandatory for visible manufacturing damage.
- If replacement stock is unavailable, issue store credit of equal value.

## Customer Complaint Handling
1. Acknowledge the issue and apologize briefly.
2. Offer one of: remake, refund, or store credit.
3. Capture the incident in the shift log with order ID.
4. Escalate repeated equipment-related complaints to the duty manager.
